<p>This year, at the 2025 Pro-Enterprise Panel-Singapore Business Federation
(PEP-SBF) Awards<u> held on 25 November 2025</u>, TradeTrust won the Pro-Enterprise
Impact &amp; Partnership Award for our project on <a href="https://www.tradetrust.io/beijing-and-singapore-launch-interoperable-digital-documents-against-payment-trade-corporation/" class="Hyperlink TrackedChange TrackChangeHyperlinkInstruction SCXW163969043 BCX0" rel="noreferrer noopener" target="_blank"><u>Interoperable Digital Documents Against Payment (D/P)</u></a>.&nbsp;</p>
<p>We are honoured to receive the award, which recognises projects that exemplify
strong partnerships between businesses and regulatory agencies, close collaboration
across the public and private sectors, and clear, impactful regulatory
outcomes.</p>
<p>Through the PEP-SBF Awards, Singapore champions deeper public-private
collaboration to streamline regulations and encourage innovative approaches
that drive strong business growth. This recognition affirms TradeTrust’s
role in advancing more business-friendly regulatory environments through
impactful, cross-sector partnerships while underscoring IMDA’s ongoing
commitment to fostering innovation-friendly partnerships that drive efficiency,
transparency, and growth in global trade.&nbsp;</p>
