<p>Collaboration is key in an interconnected world. With Interoperability
as this year’s theme, Appreciation Day 2026 was a significant occasion
to celebrate and reaffirm the importance of strengthening our connections
and building trust in our infrastructure.</p>
<p>A heartfelt thank you to all our guest speakers for sharing their valuable
experiences. Alongside insightful presentations from thought leaders and
an interoperability demonstration, we also celebrated key milestones that
marked our progress in enabling the seamless transfer of trusted electronic
Bills of Lading (eBLs) in global digital trade.</p>
<p>This included our TradeTrust Partners Awards, a special recognition for
those who have contributed significantly to advancing interoperability
and global trade and the launch of the TradeTrust Readiness Programme to
support businesses in adopting interoperable digital trade solutions.</p>
<p>As interoperable systems move from experimentation to real-world adoption,
TradeTrust will continue to work with partners to build an open, trusted,
and connected future for global trade.</p>
<p>Once again, thank you to all those who made the event a success. Your
support helps us build a better future for digital trade.</p>
